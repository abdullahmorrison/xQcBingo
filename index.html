Description:

This is a website where you can play a bingo game about the Twitch streamer xQcOW.

Instructions:

watch xQc's stream. If xQc does any of the actions stated on the board, add a chip on the action he did.

How to add/remove a chip:

Click on the tile that contains the action that xQc commited.

How to win (Bingo!):

You win if you have a full row, column, or diagonal filled with chips.

Note:

Progress is not saved. Refreshing will cause the board to reset.
